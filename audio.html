MIDIs!

To preface this, please for the love of god don't open these in something like FL Studio and get mad when they don't play correctly! Install the CoolSoft Midi Mapper, an OPL3 MIDI emulator, preferably one that uses the Windows MSGS patches, and use a VST MIDI driver for the S-YXG50 (Yamaha made it free!) Find ways to properly emulate the rest, and for MSGS just use good old WMP.


If you want to use any of these MIDIs in your projects, email me and give me credit where it's due.
